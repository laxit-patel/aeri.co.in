Welcome To

Ahmedabad Engineering Research Institute

AERI Laboratories (P) Ltd. was established at modest scale in 1991 to provided Testing facilities and services in all type of Civil engineering Material testing in Lab and as well as field. Over the past one decade, the laboratory expanded its analytical facilities to keep pace with the requirements of Industry and the society. It is well Established Laboratories in Gujarat from since beginning to till date, its well equipped with highly sophisticated instruments, managed by highly qualified professionals and also providing Analytical Services in the various fields.
AERI has been acknowledged as a trustworthy organization for its Testing Calibration Services. We have gained the trust of numerous customers, across the Gujarat, by our Customer-oriented approach and ethical practices. As AERI is experienced with making adaptations for custom projects, specialized requests such as non-standard tests or setups are welcomed.
